Our Story

About Us

The Plush Atelier was born from a shared passion for creativity, connection, and making moments that truly matter.

As a husband-and-wife team, we were drawn to creating something that felt fun, creative, and meaningful. We've always loved hosting, working with kids, and creating moments that make people smile — so when we discovered the idea of plush-making parties, it instantly felt like the perfect fit.

We wanted to offer an experience that's hands-on, memorable, and a little different — something kids can truly enjoy and feel proud of.

At The Plush Atelier, it's not just about making stuffed animals. It's about creating something with your own hands, sharing the moment with others, and leaving with a memory you can hold onto.
